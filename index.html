ABOUT US

तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय (Tamso Ma Jyotirgamaya) meaning “From ignorance, lead us to knowledge”


The motto of the University is taken from Brhadaranyaka Upanishad – I.III.28. The second line of the Pavamana Mantra explains how to lead the life towards knowledge from ignorance that obscures our mind in understanding the reality. As the only remedy from darkness is light, the only remedy from ignorance is knowledge.
